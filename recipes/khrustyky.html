Khrustyky


Description

These crispy fried cookies are popular in Ukraine. They're coated in a light dusting of powdered sugar and are so crunchy and absolutely irresistible! Khrustyky are a great party dessert because you can put out a few bowls and let people snack on it throughout the party!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium eggs
  • 300 grams wheat flour
  • 2 teaspoons rum
  • a pinch of salt
  • 700 ml vegetable oil
  • some powdered sugar
  • baking powder

How to cook

  1. Sift the wheat flour with baking powder, add salt and sugar. Add 2 eggs and mix, adding milk, melted butter and rum. Knead elastic dough, gradually adding the remaining flour.
  2. Wrap the dough in cling film and leave to rest at room temperature for 30-40 minutes. Roll the dough out into a thin layer with a thickness of 2-5 mm. Make a small rectangle, then make a small longitudinal incision in the middle and pull 2 corners of the dough through it.
  3. Heat the oil in a deep frying pan and fry over medium heat for 1-2 minutes until golden brown, and then place on paper towels.
  4. Sift with powdered sugar before serving.
